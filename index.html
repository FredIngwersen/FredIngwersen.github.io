A picture says more than a thousand words.

When looking for your next home a lot of factors come into play – but ultimately it comes down to; does it feel right for me? Could I see myself live here?

If you’re like me, that’s a tough question in and of itself. Nevertheless, I have a good idea of my preferences when it comes to style, feel, spaciousness, natural lighting and small kitchens – I just can’t couldn’t search for homes using this knowledge.

Shelta uses machine learning algorithms to match visual feel to homes. You swipe through images; tell us which ones you like – and we find great matches for you as soon as they appear in the market.
